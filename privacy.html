Privacy Policy

KZ-Export-Tool · Last updated: July 5, 2026

KZ-Export-Tool is a private application used solely by its operator to manage the operator's own eBay listings, including inventory status and pricing. It is not a public service and has no external users.

Information the application accesses

Through eBay's official API, the application accesses only the operator's own eBay account data (such as the operator's own listings, prices, and order details). This access is used exclusively for the operator's own business operations.

What the application does not do

The application does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information of any third party. It never accesses or stores any eBay user's password. Access to the operator's eBay account is granted only through eBay's official OAuth authorization process.

Data storage and security

Access tokens obtained through eBay's authorization process are stored securely on the operator's own computer and are never shared with any third party. The operator can revoke the application's access at any time from eBay account settings (Sign in and security → Third-party app access).

Contact

For any questions regarding this application, please contact the operator through eBay's messaging system.

This application is operated by an individual eBay seller for personal business use only.
